About
Keeping up with the global trend of revolutionizing finance through technology, the Government of Maharashtra state has formulated a Fintech policy that envisages to develop Mumbai as a FinTech hub for India in the coming years. This hub would house several financial institutions and Fintech startups and would position Mumbai as a leading center for financial services innovation.
FinTech is the emerging domain of transformation of the banking and financial services industry through technological intervention and includes developments such as digital banking, alternative lending, digital payments, etc. An imminent FinTech revolution has the potential to create several skilled jobs in the country.
With this goal in mind, the FinTech Educational Platform (FEP) was launched, which hosts e-learning modules covering various sought after technical, functional and miscellaneous skill-sets. The FEP aims to prepare potential job seekers and aspiring entrepreneurs to be proficient in the skills and domain knowledge required in the FinTech industry in order to create an employable FinTech workforce.
The FEP hosts 6 courses in the FinTech and associated domains currently with the launch of several more eminent. The courses have been tailor made for the Indian audience and developed in collaboration with several leading banking industry players. Post course completion, the reader can take an assessment- on successful completion of which they would earn a digital certificate from the government of Maharashtra.
The courses that are currently available on the platform are:
1. Introduction to FinTech
2. Technology in FinTech
3. Digital Banking
4. Digital Payments
5. Digital Lending
6. Security, regulation and compliance
The readers can also gain valuable insights from industry leaders through a series of 6 videos podcasts, one for each course.
