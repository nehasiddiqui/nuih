Frequently Asked Questions
1. Course Structure of the FEP courses
All courses in the FEP are self-paced, allowing the reader, and not the instructor, to be in the driver’s seat and control their learning pace. This provides higher flexibility and customization of the learning to match individual reader needs. As a result, there would be higher reflection into and retention of the concepts by the reader. Each course is followed by a timed examination to assess the conceptual understanding of the courses by the reader. This is a progressive test that consists of multiple choice type questions of varying difficulty levels from different sections of the course . Passing the test marks the successful completion of the course, which warrants an online certification from the Government of Maharashtra.
One of the defining features of the courses on the FEP is the adaptation of FinTech concepts and their applications in the Indian perspective. Each new concept introduced is followed by multiple supporting arguments in the form of examples from the Indian financial industry. These help the reader better relate to the real-life applications of the concept and its impact on the industry.
Another key highlight of the courses is the ‘industry snapshot’ section at the end of each course. This is a video that contains valuable insights from prominent personalities from the Indian financial or FinTech industry. The insight asks open ended and thought provoking questions to the reader and attempts to inculcate a structured approach to industry level problems as well as to developing their solutions.
2. Approach to the content of FEP courses
A key feature of the FEP, the one that makes it the most relevant to Indian readers is the approach followed for course development. Unlike other e-learning platforms, where the focus of learning is from an academic focus, the FEP courses are developed, and their content tailored, in coordination with professionals from the industry. The topics to be covered in the course modules, were selected after extensive deliberation  with professionals and influencers from the FinTech, as well as finance industry in India, to match the skills and knowledge domains that are in the most in-demand in the industry. This would help the reader learn the most relevant and applicable concepts from an industry perspective, in-turn making these readers desirable candidates for employment in FinTech roles in the Indian financial industry. The FEP thus acts as a stepping stone, for students and professionals alike, to enter the FinTech field.
3. Few features of the FEP courses
Use of infographics, videos, intuitive design and easy navigation to facilitate learning-
These features are a must have as they give e-learning courses their characteristic distinction from traditional text-book based learning.They greatly increase the ease of understanding and the level of retention of concepts compared to plain text- based learning.
Easily accessible and free content-
The FEP courses are available to everyone free of cost.
Multiple levels of proficiency for different target groups-
The FEP consists of courses of varying difficulty and proficiency levels to cater to the learning needs of a wide range of target audiences.
Simple language, multiple supporting examples and logical flow of course content-
These features are essential for a deeper understanding of difficult concepts and to improve comprehension. The FEP courses are based on an approach to present the course content in the simplest and most logical way possible.
Channels of delivery-
The FEP is device agnostic-it is available in the form of a mobile app as well as website for improved accessibility.

For senior year engineering and management students, the FEP is the only among e-learning platforms, that has the most relevance to an Indian reader,because it is recommended by both academia and the industry and certified by the government.
